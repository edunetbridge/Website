Why SkillBridge

Built for the children
everyone else ignored.

Africa's edtech boom passed deaf children by. SkillBridge was built from scratch to change that.

01

Africa-First, Always

Built for Nigerian Sign Language from day one — not a translation of a Western product. Real language, real curriculum, real relevance for African deaf children and families.

02

Works Without Internet

Designed for Nigeria's connectivity reality. Download lessons over WiFi — then everything works offline. Video, AI practice, quizzes. No data, no problem.

03

AI That Watches and Teaches

Our on-device AI evaluates sign language in real time using the phone camera — turning passive video watching into an active, responsive learning experience.

04

Teachers Stay in Control

No sign language knowledge required. The teacher dashboard gives full visibility over every student — who is thriving, who needs help, and exactly what to do next.

05

Any Budget Phone

Optimized for affordable Android devices. No tablets, no expensive hardware — just the phone that's already in a parent's pocket across Nigeria.

06

Curriculum-Aligned

Lessons follow Nigeria's official primary school curriculum — literacy, numeracy, science, social studies — so every child learns what they need to succeed in school and life.

How It Works

Simple for children.
Powerful for teachers.

A structured four-step learning journey that keeps deaf children progressing every single day.

1

Watch the Lesson

Short 2–5 min NSL video lessons taught by trained deaf educators. Downloadable, offline-ready, curriculum-aligned.

📹 NSL Video
2

Practice with AI

Camera activates. Child signs back what they learned. On-device AI evaluates and gives instant feedback — no internet needed.

🤟 AI Recognition
3

Take the Quiz

End-of-module quizzes test real comprehension. Scores sync to the teacher dashboard automatically when back online.

📝 Assessments
4

Teacher Guides

Dashboard shows every student's progress. Teacher assigns next lessons, spots who's falling behind, takes action — all without knowing sign language.

Features

Everything a deaf child needs
to succeed in school.

SkillBridge is a complete learning ecosystem, not just a video player.

📹

NSL Video Lessons

Short, engaging lessons taught entirely in Nigerian Sign Language by trained deaf educators. Downloadable, offline-ready.

🤟

AI Sign Recognition

MediaPipe + TensorFlow Lite runs fully on-device. Evaluates signs in real time, no internet needed. Works on budget Android.

📊

Progress Tracking

Every lesson, quiz, and practice session logged. Students see their own growth. Teachers see the full class picture.

📋

Lesson Assignment

Teachers push specific lessons to individual students or the whole class. Appears instantly in the student's app.

🚦

At-Risk Alerts

Traffic light system flags students falling behind in real time — so no child slips through the cracks unnoticed.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent NSL Course

90% of deaf children have hearing parents. Our parent module teaches basic NSL so families can communicate and support learning at home.
